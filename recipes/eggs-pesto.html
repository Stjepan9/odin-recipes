Eggs-pesto

Description

Eggs-pesto is a simple yet flavorful dish that pairs creamy eggs with vibrant pesto sauce.
The eggs, often scrambled or fried, are coated with a luscious layer of basil pesto, made from fresh basil, garlic, pine nuts, Parmesan cheese, and olive oil.
