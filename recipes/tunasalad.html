Tuna salad

Description

A tuna salad is a light yet satisfying dish, typically made by combining canned tuna with a variety of fresh ingredients.
The tuna is usually mixed with mayonnaise or Greek yogurt to create a creamy base, though some recipes use olive oil or mustard for a lighter option.
Classic add-ins include finely chopped celery, onions, pickles, or relish for crunch and tang, as well as hard-boiled eggs for richness.
Fresh herbs like parsley or dill can brighten up the flavor, while a squeeze of lemon juice adds a refreshing citrus note.
Often served on a bed of leafy greens, in a sandwich, or as a filling for wraps, a tuna salad is versatile, easy to make, and packed with protein.

Ingredients

  • Tuna
  • Eggs
  • Avocado
  • Sourdough bread
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Cook your eggs
  2. Place avocado on top of bread
  3. Put tuna on top of bread
  4. Put eggs on top of tuna
  5. Salt and pepper
