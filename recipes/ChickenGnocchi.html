ChickenGnocchi

Description

This chicken gnocchi dish is a comforting blend of tender, juicy chicken and pillowy-soft gnocchi, all simmered in a creamy, flavorful sauce.
The rich, savory broth is infused with garlic, herbs, and a touch of Parmesan, creating a perfect balance of creaminess and depth that coats every bite.
Ideal for a cozy dinner, it combines hearty ingredients in a satisfying, soul-warming meal.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Gnocchi
  • Olive oil
  • Butter
  • Onion
  • Heavy cream
  • Herbs

Steps

  1. Season chicken
  2. Heat water
  3. Cook chicken on a pan
  4. Put gnocchi in heated water
  5. Drain cooked gnocchi from water
  6. Add cream to the pan and stir
  7. Combine gnocchi, cream and chicken
  8. Add salt and pepper
  9. Serve
